ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Andres Betancur, and I am a passionate 3D animator with extensive experience in the digital animation industry. My journey into the world of animation began two years ago and since then I have been immersed in the exciting and creative universe of animation. My primary skill lies in mastering cutting-edge software such as Autodesk Maya, Blender, Cinema 4D and other 3D animation programs. Throughout my career, I have worked on a variety of projects, from advertising and visual animations for video games to animated films and shorts. My love of technology and art come together in my daily work as I design characters, settings and visual effects that come to life on the screen. One of the most rewarding aspects of my job is the ability to turn abstract concepts into impactful images and animations. My approach is always to maintain a balance between technique and narrative, ensuring that each project tells a compelling story through the universal language of animation. In addition to my experience in the professional field, I also love to share my knowledge with others. I have had the privilege of giving conferences and workshops on 3D animation in the cities of Bogota, Medellin and Bucaramanga, and I am excited by the opportunity to continue contributing to the creative community. When I'm not in front of my computer, I love to get inspired by exploring new technologies and animation techniques. I also enjoy film, music, and reading, as these art forms continue to nourish my creativity and perspective. If you are interested in working together on a creative project or just want to connect with a passionate 3D animator, don't hesitate to contact me! I am excited about the opportunities the future has in store and eager to continue exploring the endless world of 3D animation. Thank you for visiting my profile and learning a little more about me and my passion for 3D animation!
